Food inspired by Travel:


Our inspiration comes from different countries around the world. We focus in typical food and high quality organic products.


Cold Drinks:

Here at TodoMundo we have a big assortment of cold drinks...from soft bottled drinks to cold coffees as frappino, ice latte, cold brew as iced teas.

Hot Drinks:

Coffee is our passion! Here at TodoMundo we make great coffes such espresso, capuccino, caffé latte and flat white. We also have a variety of teas. All of our coffee beans are organic and come from countries such Brazil, Colombia, Guatemala, Honduras and Indonesia.

Foods and Snacks:

We serve a big range of hot and cold dishes, as for example,Australian pies, Brazilian pao de queijo and Scandinavian cold salads. It's also worth to try our French croissants and macarrons, Italian cannoli and of course, our Swedish cinnamon buns!
